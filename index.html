About

Howdy! Welcome to Dojo.Shrub, a small hub of things related to changing the world through teaching coding literacy ;) Most of this is supplemental content for Coding Dojo Students, but the big draw is the current curriculum calendar outlining where you should be if you are in my python stack. Below that you will find some resources geared towards students at different stages of their coding dojo experience. While this section is primarily just links to great existing content, below that you will find some content related to Dojo Side-Projects or me trying to synthesize some broader things into smaller, useful enough to apply, tidbits.
