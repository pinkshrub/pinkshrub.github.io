About

Howdy! Welcome to Dojo.Shrub, a small hub of resources for those interested in the dojo life. This is one of the hubs in the larger shrubwork, and it is focused on offering advice, tips, and information for students considering, taking, or having finished Coding Dojo's curriculum. You'll find a calendar for the current stack that I am teaching, outlining relevant details. In addition to the calendar, a collection of links for those that are preparing for the dojo, currently going through the program, or have recently graduated is available. These are mostly articles and resources to 3rd party apps that many have found helpful. Lastly, there are some topics that are frequently brought up at dojo but not covered too in-depth. You can find some thoughts from me about them in the Olio section. Last but not least, if you're here for some contact info you'll find that at the bottom. Thanks!
